Introduction

If you don't know what the NFL is, you probably aren't American, but I will give you a brief explanation. The NFL, or National Football league, is a football league that has 32 teams, the oldest being the Arizona Cardinals and the newest being the Houston Texans, who compete to win the Super Bowl every year.

"We unite people and inspire communities in the joy of the game by delivering the world's most exciting sports and entertainment experience." -The NFL

My Favorite Team

Well, I know you've been wondering what my favorite team is. Great question! The Patriots. I know what you're thinking: Ryan, you just showed me that the Patriots are the most successful franchise in the NFL, you must be a bandwagon. I understand. I'm not a Patriots fan because of our success, or because we had the best player of all time, and although those things are nice, it's because the New England area is where a lot of my family is from, and everyone likes the Celtics, Bruins, Red Sox, and Patriots. I personally don't care much about the NBA, MLB, or NHL though.
